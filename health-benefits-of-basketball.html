4>Health Benefits of Basketball

Basketball is a dynamic sport that builds stamina from the short sprints required from running up and down the court. Jumping to score a shot, take a lay-up, or grab a rebound help build musuclar endurance. Basketball is also good for mental health, as it requires hard work, dedication, and self-discipline. Not only will basketball help you burn calories, it also helps your bone health.
