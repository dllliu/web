Where is Basketball Popular?

The inclusion of basketball in the Olympic Games beginning in 1936 has helped boost the popularity of basketball across the world, as well as the televised professional basketball games from professional basketball league.Basketball has caught on particularly well in Europe and China, and Spain has several basketball leagues. The other major center of European basketball is Eastern Europe, particularly in the Balkans.
