Where is Basketball Popular?

The inclusion of basketball in the Olympic Games beginning in 1936 has helped boost the popularity of basketball across the world, as well as the televised professional basketball games from professional basketball league.Basketball has caught on particularly well in Europe and China, and Spain has several basketball leagues. The other major center of European basketball is Eastern Europe, particularly in the Balkans. However, the United States is arguably the country where basketball is the most popular. From gravity-defying dunks to flashy passes, basketball has obtained a huge following. Many also hear about basketball through athletes, which have sponsorship deals with some of the largest brands in the world, like Micheal Jordan with Nike.
